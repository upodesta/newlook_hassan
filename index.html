Do you really need a professional service?

In Bangladesh, taxation policies are complex and frequently changing. A slight error on an income tax return can create major hassles. Whether you're an individual or business, tax has strict deadlines that cannot be avoided. A professional service ensures customized planning so you pay only what's legally required — and save for a safer future.

What should I consider when choosing a tax advisor?

Verify how long the firm has been in the field, the qualifications of its members, and whether the advisor is a member of the local Taxes Bar Association. Once you have satisfactory answers to these, you can proceed with confidence.

Why should I choose Hassan & Associates?

With 28+ years of experience, we focus on individual needs and treat every client as one who matters. We thoroughly study your situation and tailor advice accordingly. Although based in Dhaka, we serve clients across Bangladesh and worldwide, with a commitment to integrity, honesty, and a zero-failure track record.

How is my data and privacy protected?

Your privacy is critical. Information shared with your preparer remains strictly confidential and is never given to a third party without your written consent. All client information is maintained in a secure location with bank-level encryption and accessible only by trusted staff.

Who is responsible for the firm's activities?

Syed Hassan Ali, CEO of the firm, is the responsible person for all activities and oversees every engagement personally.

Do you offer a free initial consultation?

Yes — we provide free advice on request. Simply fill out our appointment form or call us, and one of our team members will get in touch to understand your needs.

Which areas do you serve?

Our office is in West Rampura, Dhaka, and we serve clients across all 64 districts of Bangladesh. We also support Bangladeshi clients living abroad through our online document-handling platform.
